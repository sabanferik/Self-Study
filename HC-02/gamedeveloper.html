How to Become a Game Developer

Game Development is the art of creating games and describes the design, development and release of a game. It may involve concept generation, design, build, test and release. Wheil you create a game, it is important to think about the game mechanics, rewards, player engagement and level design.

  1. Obtain a bachelor's degree.
  2. Brush up your math and physics skills.
  3. Expand your software engineering skills.
  4. Develop your storytelling skills.
  5. Build a game developer portfolio.
